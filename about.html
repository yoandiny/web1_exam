About KeyGo

Created by Yoan - STD24005 | Rindra - STD24018

What is KeyGo?

KeyGo (from "key" and "go") is a typing web app where users jump straight into action at the keyboard. It’s designed for everyone — from casual learners to typing masters — who want to improve their speed, accuracy, and concentration.

Core Features

  • Real-time typing stats: WPM (Words per Minute) & Accuracy
  • Responsive design for desktop and mobile (Except for the profile page, which is not responsive on phones xDD)
  • Instant feedback to track your progress

Available Modes

  • Bounce mode
  • rCaSe
  • Shake
  • Reverse
  • Flash
  • Music player

Background Music

Enhance your typing experience with background music:

  • Metal
  • Heavy
  • Lead
